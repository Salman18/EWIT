About ACICT-2017

International Conference on Advance Communication, Information and Computing Technologies will be held in the silicon valley of India - Bengaluru from 5-6 May2017, will be hosted by the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, East West Institute of Technology, Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. All papers accepted by the conference will be published in World Association of Research in Science and Engineering(WARSE) Journal titled "International Journal of Computing, Communication and Networking(UCCN)".ACICT aims to bring together researchers, academicians, scholars and experts from various fields of Computer Science, Electrical and Electronics, Communication Network and Computing Technologies.
